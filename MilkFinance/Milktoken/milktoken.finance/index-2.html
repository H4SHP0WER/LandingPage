About MILK

MILK is an upgradeable ERC20 token! We have some plans for the immediate future, but beyond that, we could upgrade this token to do anything. We are bringing a fresh take to DeFi, and making a practical, safe coin that is profitable and fun to have.

How It Works

A lot of things in DeFi are needlessly complex and confusing, many things are also very boring and uninteresting, and finally, there are some profitable and even popular smart contracts that are just too much effort, and could be automated or "built in" to a token by it's developers. We are making MILK because we know that making money in crypto can be fun and easy, and people can have confidence in where they are putting their money without needing to do anything complicated or difficult, or even tedious.

Why

  • We want to get away from all the buzzwords and silly hype going around in DeFi. We believe that writing code that works the best practically is more important that writing code that people can stick a lot of marketing labels on.
